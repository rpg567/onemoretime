About Me

I grew up in San Diego, California. I surfed and ran cross country while I was in High School and often spent a lot of time at the beach.

After growing up San Diego, I came to BYU for a freshman year before I left on a mission for my church. While at BYU, I made some of my best memories with some of the greatest people I've ever met.

Here's a picture of some of us standing in front of a firework as it went off.


After a freshman year, I served a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. I ended up serving in 3 different places: Florida Ft Lauderdale, Angola Luanda, and Cincinatti, Ohio. My mission ended up being one of the best expeirences I've ever had.

After returning home, I got back to BYU and started studying Finance, which is currently what I'm up to today.
